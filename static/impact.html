Our Mission

We believe technology should be free for everyone — and that using it should make the world a better place.

Who we are

Solaris was born from a simple idea: AI is powerful, but it's locked behind paywalls that most people can't afford.

We built Solaris so anyone — anywhere in the world — can create with AI for free. No subscriptions. No credit cards. No limits on imagination.

Every time you generate an image, video or audio on Solaris, you watch a short ad. That ad pays for your creation — and 50% of what it generates goes directly to humanitarian causes around the world.

Our vision

We live in a world where the best tools belong to those who can pay. We don't think that's right. Solaris is our answer.
